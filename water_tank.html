How the Algorithm Works (Two-Pointer)

1
Start with two pointers: left = 0, right = n-1 and track maxLeft = 0, maxRight = 0.
2
At each step, whichever side has the smaller max height is the bottleneck — move that pointer inward.
3
Water at a position = maxHeight - currentHeight. If the current block is taller than max, update max (no water here).
4
Continue until left >= right. Sum all water units → that's your answer!
